About


A unique conference aiming to engage individuals, as Global Citizens, to end extreme poverty by 2030, by focusing on four core policy issues -- women and girls, vaccines, global education, and sanitation.

The Action Summit will feature an adjacent outdoor activation in Washington Square Park engaging NYU students, Summit attendees and the general public in creative, captivating activations around the core policy themes.

Building the identity of Global Citizens as everyday people committed to taking action in support of ending extreme poverty by 2030, Global Citizen Festival - The Action Summit will zero in on the importance of the next 18 months in achieving this goal.
